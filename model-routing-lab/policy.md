# Return & Refund Policy (fake, for lab use)

1. **Standard return window:** 30 days from delivery for change-of-mind returns, item must be unused/unopened in original packaging.
2. **Defective/damaged items:** eligible for refund regardless of the standard window, as long as reported within 90 days of delivery. This includes items marked "final sale" — the final-sale exclusion applies only to change-of-mind returns, not to defects.
3. **Gift cards (physical or digital):** non-refundable and non-returnable in all cases, opened or not.
4. **Wrong item shipped (fulfillment error):** always eligible for refund or replacement, regardless of return window.
5. **Missing receipt/order confirmation:** does not block a refund. Support can verify purchase via account email or payment method lookup.
6. **Third-party marketplace orders:** not eligible for refund through this support channel. Customer must be redirected to the marketplace's own support.
7. **Non-delivery (item never arrived per tracking):** handled as a separate claim from "received but defective" — do not apply partial-refund-for-defect logic to a non-delivery claim. Escalate to shipping-loss investigation.
8. **Subscription cancellations:** a cancellation is effective from the date support/system receives it. If a customer claims to have cancelled before a renewal charge but the system shows no record of the cancellation, treat as unresolved — do not assume either the charge or the cancellation claim is correct without further verification. Always stop future charges going forward once a cancellation request is received, regardless of resolving the disputed past charge.
9. **Split payment methods (store credit + card):** refunds are returned proportionally to each original payment method used, store credit first.
